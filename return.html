Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a return?

Once we receive your returned item, we typically process returns within 3-5 business days. Refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear in your account depending on your payment method and financial institution.

What items are not eligible for return?

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on intimate apparel, swimwear, earrings, and personalized/customized items unless they arrive damaged or defective. Final sale items are also not eligible for return.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

We provide free return shipping for defective or incorrect items. For other returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless you choose store credit, in which case we'll provide a prepaid return label.

Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?

Yes! We offer free exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Simply select the exchange option when initiating your return and choose the new item you'd like. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

What if my item arrives damaged or defective?

We're sorry to hear that! Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damage/defect. We'll arrange for a free return and send you a replacement or issue a full refund, including original shipping costs.
