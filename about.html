Welcome to Bettsiddy Collection

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Kenya, Nairobi, Bettysiddy collection began as a small boutique with a big dream: to make high fashion accessible without compromising on quality or ethics. What started as a single atelier has blossomed into a global brand beloved by fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

At Bettsidddy collection, we believe fashion should be an expression of individuality while respecting our planet. Each collection is carefully curated to blend timeless elegance with contemporary trends, using sustainable materials and ethical production methods. Our designs have graced runways from kenya to the rest of the world, but our true pride comes from seeing our pieces become cherished staples in your wardrobe.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to express their unique style through ethically-made, high-quality fashion that doesn't compromise the planet or people.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the fashion industry by proving that style, sustainability, and social responsibility can coexist beautifully.
