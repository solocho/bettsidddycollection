Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries about our products and services.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for store credit or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Final sale items are not eligible for return. Please contact us at returns@bettsiddy.com to initiate a return.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website. For international orders, please allow 2-3 business days for tracking to update after shipment.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide via DHL Express. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international orders may be subject to customs fees, taxes, or duties which are the responsibility of the recipient.

How can I get styling advice?

Our personal stylists are available for virtual consultations. Book an appointment through our website or email styling@chiccouture.com. You can also visit our boutique for in-person styling sessions with our fashion experts.
