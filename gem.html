OUR STORY

Bettsiddy gem Collections is dedicated to preserving and promoting Kenya's rich jewelry-making heritage. Each piece in our collection is handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

We work directly with local craftsmen and women, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Our jewelry incorporates authentic Kenyan materials like recycled brass, beads, and semi-precious stones.

By choosing Bettsiddy gem, you're not just acquiring beautiful jewelry - you're supporting Kenyan artisans and keeping cultural traditions alive.
